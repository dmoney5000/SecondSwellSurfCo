FAQ

Quick answers

Why is everything by appointment?
Because we run this out of a real working garage in Cocoa Beach, not a retail floor — the same way most great board operations started. Appointments mean your drop-off takes ten minutes, your board gets actual attention, and our overhead stays low enough to keep repairs from $45 and boards from $150. Text (321) 555-0199 and we'll find a window, usually same or next day.
How much does surfboard repair cost in Cocoa Beach?
Minor ding repairs start at $45, cracked rails at $85, fin box replacements at $95, and buckled or snapped boards at $200. Every job gets a written quote before work begins — see the full repair price list.
Do you repair epoxy boards and soft tops?
Yes. We work on polyester (PU) and epoxy (EPS) construction, including most soft tops with structural damage. Epoxy requires different resin and cure times, so turnaround can run a day or two longer.
Will you buy my used surfboard?
Often, yes — we buy boards outright, offer trade-in credit, or consign them on an 80/20 split. Bring it by or send photos and dimensions through our sell your board page.
Do you offer surf lessons for beginners?
Yes — group, semi-private, private, and kids camps, taught by licensed, CPR-certified instructors with boards included. Lessons start at $75 and every one earns credit toward a used board. Details on our surf lessons page.
I'm visiting Cocoa Beach and dinged my board. Can you rush it?
Tell us your departure date and we'll do everything we can — many small repairs can turn around in 24–48 hours for a rush fee. Worst case, we'll set you up with a used board so you don't miss a session.
