Getting here

Directions

From the Cocoa Beach Pier

Head south on A1A about a mile, turn right onto N Orlando Ave — we're on the left with the board racks out front. Five minutes, door to door.

From Orlando / 528

Take the Beachline (528) east to A1A south through Cape Canaveral into Cocoa Beach. About 50 minutes from downtown Orlando — bring your board, leave with it fixed the same week.

From Satellite Beach & Melbourne

A1A north along the ocean — about 25 minutes from Satellite Beach. Drop your board on the way to work, pick it up on the way home.
