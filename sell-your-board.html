Three ways to part with a board

Pick what matters: speed, value, or zero effort

Option 01 · Fastest

Sell outright for cash

Book a quick evaluation at the garage and we'll make an offer on the spot. Cash in hand in ten minutes. Best for boards in solid shape that you just want gone today.

Option 02 · Best value if upgrading

Trade in for store credit

Trade credit runs 10–20% higher than our cash offer, applied instantly to anything on the used rack. The cheapest possible way to change up your quiver.

Option 03 · Highest payout

We photograph, list, display, and sell your board in-store and online. You set the floor price with us, keep 80% of the sale, and we handle every buyer conversation. Boards typically sell in 2–6 weeks.

What we take

What sells (and what doesn't)

Bring us

Shortboards, fish, grovelers, mid-lengths, longboards, and quality soft tops. Known shapers and brands move fastest — Lost, Channel Islands, Pyzel, Rusty, JS, and local Space Coast shapers like Ricky Carroll always have buyers waiting. Watertight repairs are fine when they're disclosed.

We'll usually pass on

Waterlogged or open-dinged boards (though our repair bench can fix that first — repaired boards consign at a much better price), big-box foamies in rough shape, and boards with undisclosed structural damage. We'll always tell you why, and what it would take to make it sellable.
